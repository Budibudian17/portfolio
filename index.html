Who am I?

Hi! I'm Hilmi Farrel Firjatullah, a student at SMKN 01 Depok majoring in Software and Game Development. I'm passionate about building web applications and developing 3D games. I've worked on real-world projects like CiptaLife, a health management system for the Depok City Health Department, where I helped digitize patient services and manage health data. This experience sharpened my skills in web development, teamwork, and problem-solving.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and PHP, I'm always eager to learn new technologies like Unity and Blender. Outside of coding, I'm active in ROHIS, enjoy football (huge Cristiano Ronaldo fan!), and love exploring 3D modeling as a hobby. My dream is to one day work with world-class companies like Hoyoverse or RIOT Games and contribute to innovative digital experiences.
